# Privacy Policy for KnowTon Mobile App

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

KnowTon Platform ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Register for an account
- Make a purchase
- Contact customer support
- Participate in surveys or promotions

Personal information may include:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Wallet address (for cryptocurrency transactions)
- Payment information
- Profile information

### Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, we automatically collect certain information, including:
- Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- IP address
- App usage data (features used, time spent, interactions)
- Location data (with your permission)
- Crash reports and performance data

### Content Information
When you upload or interact with content:
- Content files and metadata
- Purchase history
- Viewing history
- Ratings and reviews
- Comments and interactions

### Financial Information
For transactions:
- Payment method information (processed by third-party payment processors)
- Transaction history
- Wallet addresses and transaction hashes (for blockchain transactions)
- Investment portfolio data

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

### Provide and Maintain Services
- Create and manage your account
- Process transactions and payments
- Deliver purchased content
- Provide customer support
- Send transactional notifications

### Improve and Personalize
- Personalize content recommendations
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Develop new features and services

### Security and Fraud Prevention
- Detect and prevent fraud
- Ensure platform security
- Comply with legal obligations
- Enforce our terms of service

### Marketing and Communications
- Send promotional materials (with your consent)
- Notify you about new features or content
- Conduct surveys and research
- Send push notifications (with your permission)

## How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

### Service Providers
- Payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, Alipay, WeChat Pay)
- Cloud storage providers (AWS, IPFS)
- Analytics providers (Firebase, Google Analytics)
- Customer support tools
- Email service providers

### Blockchain Networks
- Transaction data is publicly visible on blockchain networks
- Wallet addresses and transaction hashes are public
- Smart contract interactions are recorded on-chain

### Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to lawful requests from authorities
- Protect our rights and property
- Prevent fraud or illegal activities
- Protect user safety

### Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

### With Your Consent
We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:
- Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Encryption at rest (AES-256)
- Secure authentication mechanisms
- Regular security audits
- Access controls and monitoring
- Secure key management (HSM)

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

### Access and Portability
- Request access to your personal information
- Receive a copy of your data in a portable format

### Correction and Update
- Correct inaccurate information
- Update your profile information

### Deletion
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Note: Some information may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes

### Opt-Out
- Unsubscribe from marketing communications
- Disable push notifications
- Opt out of personalized advertising

### Data Processing Restrictions
- Restrict how we process your information

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:
- Provide our services
- Comply with legal obligations
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce our agreements

When information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

### Retention Periods:
- Account information: Duration of account + 7 years
- Transaction records: 7 years (legal requirement)
- Usage data: 2 years
- Marketing data: Until you opt out + 30 days
- Blockchain data: Permanent (immutable)
