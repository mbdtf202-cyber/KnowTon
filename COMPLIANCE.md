## üîê KYC/AML Policy

### Know Your Customer (KYC)

**Verification Levels**:

**Level 1: Basic** (Email + Wallet)
- Maximum transaction: $10,000 USD equivalent
- Required information:
  - Email address (verified)
  - Wallet address
  - Country of residence
- Verification time: Instant

**Level 2: Standard** (Government ID)
- Maximum transaction: $100,000 USD equivalent
- Required information:
  - Level 1 requirements
  - Government-issued ID (passport/driver's license)
  - Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  - Selfie verification
- Verification time: 24-48 hours
- Provider: Jumio/Onfido

**Level 3: Enhanced** (Full Due Diligence)
- Unlimited transactions
- Required information:
  - Level 2 requirements
  - Source of funds documentation
  - Enhanced background checks
  - Beneficial ownership disclosure (for entities)
- Verification time: 3-5 business days
- Manual review required

### Anti-Money Laundering (AML)

**Transaction Monitoring**:
- Real-time screening against OFAC/UN sanctions lists
- Automated suspicious activity detection
- Threshold-based alerts ($10k+ transactions)
- Pattern analysis for structuring/smurfing

**Suspicious Activity Indicators**:
- Rapid high-value transactions
- Unusual geographic patterns
- Inconsistent with user profile
- Known high-risk wallet interactions
- Mixing service usage

**Reporting**:
- Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs) filed as required
- Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs) for $10k+ cash equivalents
- Cooperation with law enforcement
- Record retention: 5 years minimum

### Counter-Terrorist Financing (CTF)
- Screening against terrorist watch lists
- Enhanced due diligence for high-risk jurisdictions
- Transaction blocking for sanctioned entities
- Immediate reporting of suspected terrorist financing

## üìã Securities Compliance

### Token Classification

**Utility Token (KNOW)**:
- Primary purpose: Platform governance and access
- Howey Test analysis: Not an investment contract
- No profit expectations marketed
- Functional from launch
- **Status**: Utility token position

**Fractional NFTs**:
- May constitute securities depending on structure
- Reg D exemption for accredited investors
- Reg S for non-US persons
- Reg A+ for broader retail access
- **Approach**: Case-by-case analysis

**IP Bonds**:
- Likely securities (debt instruments)
- Offered only to accredited/qualified investors
- Proper disclosure documents
- Compliance with securities laws
- **Status**: Securities treatment

### Offering Compliance

**Reg D (US Accredited Investors)**:
- Form D filing with SEC
- Accredited investor verification
- No general solicitation (506(b)) or with verification (506(c))
- State blue sky compliance

**Reg S (Non-US Persons)**:
- Offshore transactions only
- No directed selling efforts in US
- Appropriate legends and restrictions
- Category 2 or 3 compliance

**Reg A+ (Mini-IPO)**:
- SEC qualification required
- Offering circular preparation
- Ongoing reporting obligations
- Up to $75M raise limit
